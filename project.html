Available Scholarships

Merit-Based Scholarship

Awarded to students demonstrating exceptional academic achievement, leadership, and community involvement.

  • Amount: $3,000
  • Deadline: July 1, 2025
  • Eligibility: Minimum GPA 3.5, leadership experience required
  • Application Process: Submit transcript, personal statement, and recommendation letters

Need-Based Grant

Financial assistance for students with significant financial need to pursue their education goals.

  • Amount: $4,000
  • Deadline: August 15, 2025
  • Eligibility: Family income below threshold, must provide financial documents
  • Application Process: Fill financial aid form and submit tax documents

Women in STEM Scholarship

Supporting female students pursuing degrees in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.

  • Amount: $5,000
  • Deadline: September 30, 2025
  • Eligibility: Female students enrolled in STEM majors
  • Application Process: Essay on contributions to STEM field required

Community Service Award

Recognizes students who have made significant contributions to their community through volunteer work.

  • Amount: $2,500
  • Deadline: October 20, 2025
  • Eligibility: Minimum 100 volunteer hours documented
  • Application Process: Submit volunteer records and recommendation letters
